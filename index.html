02 — Services

Our Marine Survey
Services

Expert cargo and marine surveyors ensuring accuracy and reliability on every project — onboard and offshore across all Saudi ports.

01Tow Approval Survey+

Comprehensive inspections to approve towing operations and ensure safe transit of towed vessels and structures.

02Cargo Securing Survey+

Verification that cargo is properly secured to prevent damage during transport and meets all regulatory requirements.

03Draft Surveys+

Accurate quantity assessments based on vessel draft readings for bulk cargo at load and discharge ports.

04Bunker On-Hire / Off-Hire Surveys+

Precise bunker quantity measurement at commencement and completion of charter to protect owner and charterer interests.

05Bunker Quantity Survey+

Independent verification of fuel quantity delivered during bunkering operations at all Saudi ports.

06Damage & Claims Surveys+

Thorough investigation and documentation of cargo damage to support insurance claims and legal proceedings.

07Cargo Supervision & Monitoring+

Continuous oversight of loading, stowage, and discharge operations to ensure cargo integrity throughout.

08Cargo Gear Inspections & Certification+

Inspection and certification of lifting equipment and cargo gear to ensure safe operation and compliance.

09Vessel's Condition Survey+

Complete assessment of vessel condition for on/off hire, pre-purchase, and insurance evaluations.

10Heavy Lift Loading, Discharging & Supervision+

Expert supervision for complex heavy lift cargo operations ensuring safety and compliance at every stage.

11Lashing & Securing Certification+

Certification that lashing and securing arrangements meet all IMO and flag state regulatory requirements.

12Quality & Quantity Surveys+

Independent assessment of cargo quality and quantity at load and discharge ports to protect client interests.

13Cargo Discharge & Tally Surveys+

Accurate tally and count of cargo discharged to ensure complete delivery and identify any shortfalls.

14Hull Inspection Surveys+

Detailed inspection of vessel hull condition above and below the waterline for insurance and maintenance purposes.
